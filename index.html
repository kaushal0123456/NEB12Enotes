Welcome to the NEB Computer Science Grade 12 course! This program is designed to provide students with a robust understanding of the world of computer science. The course offers an introduction to key concepts like computer hardware, software development, algorithms, and programming languages. Throughout the course, students will gain practical experience in programming using languages such as C and will learn how to design, implement, and test software applications.

By focusing on both technical and theoretical aspects, the curriculum emphasizes the importance of computational thinking, problem-solving, and ethical considerations in technology. The course also prepares students to think critically about the ever-evolving tech industry and provides a solid foundation for advanced studies or future careers in tech.
